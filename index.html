How FITPASS Works?

With FITPASS, fitness businesses simply list their available class slots on the platform. Our user-friendly app and website then enable customers to easily discover and book these sessions at partner studios across India.

Once a booking is made, that time slot gets blocked off exclusively for the customer who reserved it. The studio receives the booking details immediately through the FITPASS Partner Portal. After the session, the studio marks the booking as completed and gets paid seamlessly by FITPASS.
